## Sensor Placement and Experimental Design

15. Krause, A., Singh, A., & Guestrin, C. (2008). "Near-Optimal Sensor Placements in Gaussian Processes: Theory, Efficient Algorithms and Empirical Studies." *Journal of Machine Learning Research*, 9, 235-284. ## Multiple-Point Statistics
